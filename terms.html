Terms of Use

  1. Commissions prices listed are for personal use only.
    • This means the artwork is for non-profit purposes.
    • If you would want to commission me for commercial purposes(merchandise, game assets, etc.), please message me so we can discuss.
  2. Do not claim the artwork as yours.
  3. Whenever using the artwork as header image, icon, etc. Please link/ credit at least one of my social media accounts on your profile/ description.

Process

  1. Feel free to contact me for a price quote before pushing through with your order as all prices listed are base prices. There will be additional costs for backgrounds, props, and complicated designs.
  2. When messaging me for a commission request please outline your request like this:
    • Commission Type: (Full Render Chibi Fullbody, Manga Render Anime Halfbody, etc.)
    • Character Reference Image:
    • (for reference sheet)Please provide descriptions/ image pegs of what you want your character to look like.
    • Desired pose(description or image) (if any):
    • Deadline(if any):
    • Do you want to remain as an anonymous commissioner or is it fine if I tag your social media when I post it?
  3. I accept payment only via Paypal.
  4. I will send a Paypal invoice to your Paypal email once you've confirmed your commission request with me.
  5. I will only start with the commission when I receive at least half of the total payment.
  6. After finishing the sketch, I will message you to check if the sketch is ok. At this stage you are allowed to ask for a maximum of 5 changes to the sketch.
  7. I will proceed on lineart and rendering after I receive the rest of the payment. Once I finish the lineart, I will not allow any more major changes(pose and clothes design). However, if I miss small details(accessories, slight changes in facial expression) feel free to tell me.
  8. When the commission request is completed no refunds are allowed. Refunds will only be allowed during the sketch stage if you are unsatisfied with the sketch. The refund given back during the sketch stage may range from 80%-100% of the total price.

By commissioning me, you agree to all the terms and conditions listed above.
